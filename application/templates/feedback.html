Get feedback

Regular information on how you're doing can help you improve, work more effectively as a team, or make the most of new skills you are picking up.

You should be getting regular feedback anyway - from you manager at one to one meetings and through reports on business processes you're part of. You can manually add or send in evidence elsewhere in this service, but this page makes it easy to ask others.

Don't wait until it's mid-year or annual review time - feedback can help at any point. And do take a few minutes to reflect on feedback when it arrives - this is one of the most important parts of the process.
